Call Congress

TIPS ON CALLING

  1. *Calling local offices is important and sometimes better than the DC office.*
  2. Leave a message if you can’t speak to somebody
  3. If all inboxes are full/phone lines down, send an email, then call back another day!

HOW TO CALL EFFECTIVELY

  1. **MAKE SURE TO LEAVE YOUR NAME AND ADDRESS (ESPECIALLY ZIP CODE)** Letting the rep know you are a constituent is very important. Calls might/probably will be disregarded if you are not a constituent. Each office tallies the number of calls they get about an issue for the Senator/Rep to consider in making their decisions. (so your call matters a lot!!!)
  2. Name the issue you are talking about
  3. Tell the representative/senator what you want them to do (vote yes/no, support this, block this, etc.)
  4. Briefly explain why you are calling about the issue
